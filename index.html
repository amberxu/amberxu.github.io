What We Do

AI4ALL is a national nonprofit working to increase diversity in artificial intelligence. We support educational programs around the country that give underrepresented high school students early exposure to humanistic AI. Our vision is a world where AI is created by people who represent the diverse general population.

Education Programs

AI4ALL partners with top universities to run programs where diverse high schoolers learn about humanistic AI.
