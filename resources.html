Make Your Own Dual Tone Images

Build your own double tone images on Photoshop

1. Make your image black and white. For this, open your image on Photoshop and go to: Images» Adjustments»Black&White, click accept.

2. Download dual tone template.

3. Open the dual tone psd template and place your black & white image in the middle of the two layers. Set opacity at 45%, save file when done.
